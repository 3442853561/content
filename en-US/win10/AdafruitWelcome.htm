Welcome to the Adafruit Starter Pack for Windows 10 IoT Core on Raspberry Pi2.

Windows 10 IoT Core is a new edition of Windows targeted towards small, embedded devices with or without screens. With Windows 10 IoT Core, you can write a Universal Windows app that is the interface and “personality” for your device. IoT core is designed to have a low barrier to entry and make it easy to build professional grade devices. It’s designed to work with a variety of open source languages and works seamlessly with Visual Studio.

Oh, and you can use it to build a light and color sensor, an internet-connected weather sensor and more. The Adafruit Starter Pack with Windows 10 IoT Core includes all you need to get started.

Check out your parts, set up your PC and Programming Environment, set up your Raspberry Pi 2 Board and you'll be ready to make your first project.
