Components Library

A growing community of makers is determined to change the way that they interact with the world around them. From automation to data collection, the possibilities are nearly endless. Below is a host of open-source libraries and APIs that, combined with the power of Windows 10 IoT Core, will allow you to quickly and easily hack your world to start working for you. Just download any of these discrete blocks of code and drop any combination of them into your project to interface easily with the devices and services that are most important to makers.
